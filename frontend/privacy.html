Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

StoryMagic AI collects minimal information to provide a personalized storytelling experience. This includes the child's first name and age group selected at the time of story creation, and the email address used during account registration (parents/guardians only). We do not collect any sensitive personal data from children.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely to:

  • Personalize stories with the child's name and age-appropriate content
  • Display reading history and progress in the Parents Dashboard
  • Improve the quality of our AI-generated stories

3. Data Storage

Story data and session preferences are stored locally in your browser (localStorage) and are never transmitted to third parties. Account data, if applicable, is stored securely. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to anyone.

4. Children's Privacy

StoryMagic AI is designed for children aged 4–8 years, used under the supervision of a parent or guardian. We comply with child online privacy principles. No personal information from children is collected beyond a first name for story personalization.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the OpenAI API to generate story content and Google Cloud Text-to-Speech for narration. These services process story prompts but do not receive personally identifiable information about your child.

6. Cookies

StoryMagic AI uses browser localStorage (not cookies) to remember your preferences and session data. No tracking cookies or advertising cookies are used.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out through our Contact page.
